Commercial Pest Control

Protect your business, your reputation and your customers. We provide professional pest management for commercial premises across Long Island, with tailored service contracts and full compliance support.

For Businesses

Why Your Business Needs Professional Pest Control

Pest infestations in a commercial setting can have serious consequences. They damage your reputation, trigger health department violations, compromise food safety standards, and can lead to costly shutdowns, failed audits and steep fines from the Suffolk County or Nassau County Department of Health.

At Barron Pest Control, we understand the unique challenges Long Island businesses face. Whether you run a restaurant in Patchogue, manage a warehouse in Hauppauge, or oversee a medical office in Garden City, we deliver discreet, effective Integrated Pest Management that keeps your operations running smoothly and your premises fully compliant.

With NYSDEC Reg #17056, 35+ years of experience, NPMA and NYPMA membership, and QualityPro certification, the Barron team has the training and expertise to handle pest control for even the most demanding commercial environments across Long Island.

Commercial Service Contracts

Our commercial pest control contracts are designed to keep your business pest-free all year round. Every contract is tailored to your specific premises and requirements.

Regular Inspections

Scheduled visits at intervals that suit your business — monthly, bi-monthly or quarterly. Each visit includes a thorough inspection of your premises, monitoring of bait stations and traps, and a detailed service report.

  • Full premises inspection
  • Monitoring station checks
  • Written service reports

Emergency Callouts

Contract customers benefit from priority emergency service. If a pest issue arises between scheduled visits, we respond the same day — 24/7, 365 days a year — to minimize disruption to your business operations.

  • Priority same-day response
  • Included in contract
  • Discreet, professional service

Compliance & Audits

We provide comprehensive documentation to help you satisfy Department of Health inspectors, food safety auditors, third-party auditors (AIB, SQF, BRC) and due diligence requirements. Full traceability of all treatments and products used.

  • Audit-ready documentation
  • Product usage records
  • Risk assessment & recommendations
