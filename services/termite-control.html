Signs You Have a Bird Problem

Pest birds can quickly become a serious issue for properties. Look out for these common signs:

Bird Droppings

Accumulations of bird droppings on walls, ledges, walkways, vehicles, signage, and building facades. Droppings are acidic and cause lasting damage to stonework and paintwork.

Nesting Materials

Twigs, feathers, and debris collecting in gutters, on ledges, on rooftops, and in roof spaces. Nests can block drainage and cause water damage.

Persistent Noise

Constant cooing, squawking, or flapping from roosting or nesting birds, particularly early in the morning and throughout the day. Noise can be especially problematic for businesses.

Blocked Gutters & Drains

Gutters, downpipes, and flat roof drains blocked by nesting materials, feathers, and droppings, leading to water overflow and potential structural damage.

Damaged Stock & Goods

For commercial premises, birds entering loading bays and warehouses can contaminate and damage stock, packaging, and stored goods with droppings and feathers.

Why Bird Control Is Essential

Pest birds cause far more damage than many people realise. Here’s why professional bird control matters:

  • !Disease transmission: Bird droppings carry over 60 transmissible diseases including ornithosis, salmonella, and E. coli. Dried droppings become airborne and can be inhaled.
  • !Slip hazards: Wet bird droppings on walkways, steps, fire escapes, and entrance areas create serious slip hazards, putting businesses at risk of liability claims.
  • !Property damage: Bird droppings are highly acidic and erode stonework, metalwork, and paintwork. Nesting materials block gutters and drainage, causing water damage.
  • !Business reputation: Bird droppings on shopfronts, restaurants, and commercial premises create an unhygienic and unprofessional appearance that can deter customers.

Our Bird Control Solutions

1

Bird Netting

High-quality, UV-stabilised bird netting installed across building facades, loading bays, courtyards, and roof spaces. Netting provides a near-invisible barrier that prevents birds from accessing problem areas.

2

Bird Spikes & Wire Systems

Stainless steel or polycarbonate bird spikes fitted to ledges, sills, parapets, and signage to prevent birds from landing and roosting. Bird wire systems provide a discreet alternative for listed and heritage buildings.

3

Hawking & Deterrents

For large or persistent bird problems, we can arrange specialist hawking services where trained birds of prey are used to naturally disperse pest birds from your site.

4

Cleaning & Guano Removal

We provide specialist guano (bird dropping) removal and biocidal cleaning. This is essential for health and safety compliance, particularly in commercial settings where droppings can harbour dangerous pathogens.
