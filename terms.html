Service Terms

All pest control work is carried out by qualified technicians with 35+ years of experience and full NYSDEC licensing in pest management. Our technicians are members of the National Pest Management Association (NPMA) and the New York Pest Management Association (NYPMA), and QualityPro certified.

All treatments are carried out in compliance with EPA and NYSDEC regulations, using Integrated Pest Management (IPM) methods and in accordance with current industry best practice.

We will carry out a thorough inspection of your property before commencing any treatment and will explain the recommended course of action, including any preparation you may need to undertake and any risks or precautions associated with the treatment.

Quotes and Pricing

  • All quotes are provided free of charge and without obligation.
  • Quotes are valid for 30 days from the date of issue unless otherwise stated.
  • Prices include all applicable taxes.
  • There are no hidden fees. The price we quote is the price you pay.
  • If the scope of work changes upon inspection (for example, a larger infestation than initially described), we will discuss any additional costs with you before proceeding.

Payment Terms

Payment is due on completion of the work unless otherwise agreed in writing prior to the commencement of services. For larger or ongoing contracts, alternative payment arrangements may be agreed in advance.

We accept payment by check, cash, credit card and electronic transfer. Payment details will be provided on your invoice.

Cancellations

We require a minimum of 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling of booked appointments. If you need to cancel or rearrange, please contact us as soon as possible so we can offer the slot to another customer.

For emergency callouts, once a technician has been dispatched to your property, the callout fee is non-refundable. This is because we have already committed time and resources to attending your property.

Guarantees

We stand behind the quality of our work and offer the following guarantees:

  • Rodent treatments: Follow-up visits are included as standard to monitor bait stations, check for ongoing activity and ensure the infestation is fully resolved.
  • Wasp nest treatments: Wasp nest removals and treatments are guaranteed. If the nest remains active after treatment, we will return at no additional charge.
  • Guarantees are subject to the customer following any advice and recommendations provided by the technician, including proofing and hygiene measures.

Access Requirements

To enable us to carry out our services safely and effectively, the customer must:

  • Provide safe and unobstructed access to all areas requiring treatment, including attics, basements, crawlspaces, garages, sheds and yard areas where applicable.
  • Ensure all pets are secured in a separate area away from the treatment zone during the visit and for any period specified by the technician afterwards.
  • Inform us of any relevant health conditions, allergies or sensitivities that may be affected by pest control treatments.
  • Follow any preparation instructions provided before the treatment visit (for example, clearing areas, covering fish tanks or removing food items).
