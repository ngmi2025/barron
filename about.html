Meet the Owner

About Barron Pest Control

Barron Pest Control was founded on a simple mission: to deliver honest, effective pest control with the personal touch that only a family-owned, locally operated business can offer. Based right here on Long Island, we’ve spent more than 35 years helping homes and businesses across Suffolk County, Nassau County and The Hamptons solve pest problems of every size.

Our team brings decades of hands-on experience and holds full NYSDEC licensing (Reg #17056) in pest management. We’re proud members of the National Pest Management Association (NPMA), the New York Pest Management Association (NYPMA) and the National Wildlife Control Operators Association (NWCOA), and we’re QualityPro certified — the pest control industry’s mark of excellence for training, ethics and environmental stewardship.

Every technician on the Barron team is background checked, fully insured and trained in Integrated Pest Management (IPM) — an eco-friendly, science-based approach that puts your family, pets and property first. Whether it’s a single wasp nest in Huntington or a multi-location commercial contract across Long Island, every job gets the same level of care.

When you call Barron, you reach real people who know your neighborhood. Our experts will visit your property, carry out the treatment and follow up to make sure the problem is completely resolved. That’s the Barron difference — no call centers, no subcontractors, just honest, professional pest-free living delivered by a family-owned team that genuinely cares.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by four simple principles that set us apart from the big-box national chains.

Family-Run

We’re a genuine family-owned business, not a franchise or a call-center chain. When you call Barron, you reach a real Long Island neighbor — that means personalized service, real accountability and someone who genuinely cares about solving your problem.

Honest

No hard sells, no unnecessary treatments and no hidden fees. We give you an honest assessment of your pest problem and a clear, upfront price. If we don’t think you need treatment, we’ll tell you.

Thorough

We don’t cut corners. Every job starts with a comprehensive inspection, followed by IPM-based treatment and follow-up to ensure the problem is fully resolved. We also provide exclusion and prevention advice so pests stay gone year-round.

Local

Born and raised on Long Island, we know every neighborhood from Montauk to Great Neck. That means faster response times, no travel surcharges and specialized knowledge of the seasonal pest challenges unique to Long Island homes.
