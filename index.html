About Us

Serving Long Island for Over 35 Years

Founded over 35 years ago, Barron Pest Control serves homeowners and businesses throughout Long Island. We provide full-service pest control, utilizing Integrated Pest Management (IPM) solutions to eliminate the problem at its source — not just mask the symptoms.

As a locally owned, family-run business, we pride ourselves on working directly with our customers. We’re committed to honest service, fair pricing, and the kind of personal attention that big national chains simply can’t match.

Our pest control products are environmentally friendly and safe for your family and pets. Our IPM methodology helps preserve natural habitats while keeping your home pest-free year-round. That’s the Barron difference.
