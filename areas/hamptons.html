Pest Control in The Hamptons

Barron Pest Control provides expert, discreet pest control for homes and estates throughout The Hamptons. From the wooded estates of Southampton and East Hampton to the beachfront homes of Bridgehampton, Sag Harbor, Westhampton, Montauk, Amagansett and Water Mill, we understand the unique challenges that come with summer homes, year-round residences and historic properties on Long Island's East End.

Mature landscaping, surrounding woods and open fields make Hamptons homes particularly susceptible to ticks (and Lyme disease), mosquitoes, deer mice, carpenter ants and seasonal wasp nests — pests that often surge when seasonal homes sit empty. The Barron team provides thorough, eco-friendly Integrated Pest Management (IPM) treatments across ZIP codes 11968, 11937, 11932, 11963 and 11954, with pre-arrival readiness visits, year-round protection and 24/7/365 availability. Lifelong Long Islanders, family-run for 35+ years — that's the Barron difference.
