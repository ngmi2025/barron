Pest Control on the North Shore of Long Island

Barron Pest Control provides professional, eco-friendly pest control throughout Long Island's North Shore. From the wooded estates of Oyster Bay, Cold Spring Harbor and Lloyd Harbor to the waterfront properties of Northport, Centerport, Huntington Bay, Sands Point, Kings Point and Roslyn, the North Shore's mature trees, historic homes and proximity to Long Island Sound mean homeowners face heavy pressure from rodents, termites, carpenter ants, ticks and wasps.

The Barron team understands the unique challenges of protecting older North Shore homes, wooded estates and waterfront properties from pests. We provide thorough, Integrated Pest Management (IPM) treatments that are safe for your family, pets and the local environment — with 24/7/365 availability across ZIP codes 11771, 11724, 11743, 11768, 11050 and throughout the North Shore. Lifelong Long Islanders, family-run for 35+ years, delivering pest-free living and the Barron difference.
